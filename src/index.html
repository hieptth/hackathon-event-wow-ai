What is Worldwide AI Hackathon and Web3 Summit?

The Worldwide AI Hackathon and Web3 Summit, hosted by WowDAO - the world's first decentralized autonomous organization for the AI community, is a 2-day hybrid event that brings together the AI and Web3 communities.

The event features the finalists of the Worldwide AI Hackathon, with top teams presenting their projects to esteemed judges from renowned tech giants and corporations such as Google, Microsoft, IBM, Apple, Meta, Qualcomm, JP Morgan, Stanford, Deloitte, Accenture, Siemens, and more. Additionally, there will be engaging discussions on the convergence of AI and web3. Moreover, it serves as the official launch event for WowDAO.

Attendees have a unique chance to connect with top AI executives from leading companies such as HAI Stanford, Berkeley/HAAS, Google, NVIDIA, Meta, etc, and network with AI enthusiasts, web3 experts, and blockchain professionals. Join over 5,000 AI professionals (online/offline) and business leaders for inspiring discoveries, insightful conversations, practical solutions, and valuable networking opportunities.

Topics
Worldwide AI Hackathon Ending Ceremony

The top finalists, selected from a pool of over 1,700 contestants from 88 countries, will have the opportunity to showcase their outputs in front of a distinguished panel of over 100 judges and mentors.

AI & Web3 Summit

Prominent AI executives from HAI - Stanford, NVIDIA, Meta, Google Deepmind, Berkeley Haas, Qualcomm, SAP, Oracle and other leading corps, along with esteemed web3 experts, will come together to share their invaluable insights on the latest trends in AI and Web3.

The bridge between AI and Web3

Join us as our distinguished speakers delve into the fascinating intersection of AI and Web3, exploring the possibilities and discussing potential solutions for bridging these two transformative technologies.
